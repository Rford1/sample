My Favourite Canadian Buildings

The Marine Building
A beautiful building located in Vancouver, British Columbia. Notable for its art-deco doors and elevators
The Banff Chateau Fairmont
Built by the Canadian Pacific Railway in 1888, this hotel is a stunning example of Canadian Chateauesque style.
The Parliament Buildings
Home to Canada's Parliament and Senate, the Parliament Buildings have captivated me since I was a child.
